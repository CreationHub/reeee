"300"

300 is a story of the battle at Thermopyle. Directed by Zack Snyder with producer and consult Frank Miller. The movie is about how the king of the spartans King Leonidas and 300 spartans fought against the persian king Xerxes and his army of over one million persians.

Genre: Action, Historical Fantasy, Educational

The Message or purpose of the movie is to enhance the historical event of when Leonidas lead his 300 men into battle vs the persians. It potrays the spartans as glorious warriors and the persians as weaklings, meanwhile some aspects of the movie are correct some are not. Making it a great viewing experience with a mix of myths and accurate historical events.
